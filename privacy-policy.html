Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025

Overview

Taylors Renovation Co. ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you visit our website or contact us for services.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following information when you submit a contact form or request a service estimate:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Service address
  • Details about your renovation project or request

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Respond to your service request or inquiry
  • Schedule appointments and provide estimates
  • Send service-related text messages and updates (with your consent)
  • Improve our customer service

Text Messaging (SMS)

By submitting our contact form and opting in, you consent to receive text messages from Taylor's HVAC at the number you provide, including messages sent by auto dialer. Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency varies.

To stop receiving text messages, reply STOP to any message. For help, reply HELP. No further messages will be sent after you opt out.

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal information to third parties. Your information is only used to provide and improve our services to you.

Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.
